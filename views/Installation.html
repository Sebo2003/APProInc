AP Pro Inc. Installation process

All projects begin via contact through our clients through our Gmail or phone number. Once specifications are detailed work on site begins ASAP. AC Units are bought from Ferguson and thus we only have the units when they are in stock, which is often. Large buildings that require mutliple floors of venting typically takes up a week, while smaller projects - such as closet AC's or roof units, typically take 1-3 days. Zoning is also part of our process and is accounted for during installation as well as when cooperating with electritians, plumbers, and other people on site.
