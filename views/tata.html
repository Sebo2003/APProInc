AP Pro Inc. is your go-to for everything HVAC

AP Pro Inc. is a Chicagoland area buisiness dedicated to the installation, maintance, and repair of HVAC related appliances. This includes AC units, coil installation, HVAC humidifiers, water heaters, furnaces, and boilers. We work on both indoor and outdoor units, whether it be roofing or closet. With over a decade's worth of experience, AP Pro Inc. has the competent people you need to get the job done.

Multi-brand service

AP Pro Inc's years of experience makes it easy to work with any brands of AC out there. This includes Rheem, Carrier, Gibson, American Standard and many more. Our materials are bought from Ferguson© manufacturers to ensure the best quality products go into every unit.

Service Area

AP Pro Inc. services in the general Chicagoland area and beyond. This includes all of Chicago, Evanston, Elgin, Joliet, Aurora, Naperville, and all adjacent areas.
